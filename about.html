Meet the Founder

Tess Watt is a Scottish student, tutor, and entrepreneur. Her interest in business began in her first year at university when she studied 'Information Systems in Organisations'. She later combined her interest for business with her passions for programming and teaching when she became a computing and maths tutor.

In December 2020, Tess set up a LinkedIn page called 'Techie Tessie' with the aim of making programming education as widespread and accessible as possible. In January 2021, she created the Techie Tessie YouTube channel in order to reach a wider audience.
